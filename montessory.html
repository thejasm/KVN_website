Montessory Schooling

Authentic Montessori

KVN Montessori classrooms environments are immediately recognizable. At KVN we will see all our children working work independently and in groups, often with specially designed learning materials; deeply engaged in their work; and respectful of themselves and their surroundings. As per Marie Montessori’s expectations, KVN has the best ‘prepared adults in a prepared environment’ in Thrissur. In a recently conducted survey, KVN Montessori was adjudged as the largest and oldest Montessori environment in Thrissur. The Montessori Method fosters rigorous a blissful and self-motivated growth for children in all areas of their development—cognitive, emotional, social, and physical.

  • Early years Program
  • Nursery -2.5 years to 4 years
  • Junior CASA – 4-5 years
  • Senior Casa-5-6 years
  • Elementary -6-9 years

Junior CASA – 4-5 YEARS:

The Montessori directress (teacher), child, and environment create a learning triangle. The classroom is prepared by the directress to encourage independence, freedom within limits, and a sense of order. The child, through individual choice, makes use of what the environment offers to develop himself, interacting with the directress when support and/or guidance is needed.

EARLY CHILDHOOD:

Montessori Early Childhood programs support children ages 2.5-6 in becoming independent, responsible students who love to learn.

ELEMENTARY:

Montessori Elementary programs support the development and growth of children ages 6-12.

SECONDARY:

Montessori Secondary programs are uniquely suited to support the cognitive and social development of youth ages 12-18.

  • Developing language and numeracy skills
  • Emphasis on social skills and intelligence
  • Focus on overall growth and development
  • Smooth and easy Transition to higher class

Teaching methodology

Fostering a delightful childhood experience in an ambiance of play, joy and freedom

  • Audio: Our children learn through listening to rhymes & narrations
  • Visual: They develop the concepts more with pictures, diagrams & charts
  • Kinesthetics: Fun activities and games give them hands -on learning experience
