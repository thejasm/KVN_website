    Caring For One Another For The Highest Good

Kailasanadha
Vidyanikethan

KAILASANADHA VIDYANIKETHAN is the noble vision of late Shri. N.V. Purushothaman. He envisioned preparatory environment for future leaders who will face challenges and will foster peace. The students are inculcated with the spirit of compassion and foresightedness.

KVN CBSE School provides a solid strong academic foundation and a rich extra-curricular programme supplemented by an educational programme that includes four core values including World Citizenship, Universal Values, Leadership Qualities and Forward Thinking which are part of the planned curriculum and are woven throughout the fabric of school life.

There is a great feeling of space in Our school is nestled in a bright, lush verdant 05 acre campus in 45 acres of greenery enhancing KVN’s teaching-learning which exists in the school experience, a rare environment enjoyed by a blessed few. The atmosphere opens up enough opportunities for our students to develop observation, analytical and collaborative skills every day. The buildings are all linked by pathways and turfed areas, and the whole campus is surrounded by woodland and open countryside

Principal's Message

Anita S
Principal

The aim of the school is to provide unmatched qualitative education par excellence: to develop the best that is latent in children and to encourage the child to explore and revel in the joy of learning. The endeavour is to retain smiles on the faces of the children, channelise the energies of youthfulness while sensing the beautiful connectivity between the heart, character, nation and the world.
